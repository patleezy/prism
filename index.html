Tips for better results
Be specific — include price points, product details, or specific claims to test
Use Concept Testing for new ideas, Price Sensitivity when validating a price point
Results are directional, not definitive — use to guide decisions, not replace real research
Save sessions to compare findings across multiple runs
Works for personal decisions too
Choosing between colleges, neighborhoods, or job offers — test perception, not just facts
Salary negotiation — how does this offer read to a hiring manager, a peer, or a recruiter?
Big life decisions often hinge on how others perceive the options — Prism surfaces that
Pair Prism's perception layer with real data (stats, reviews) for the full picture
